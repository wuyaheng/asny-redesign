Non Profit Services

ASNY has been providing accounting and financial services to nonprofits for more than a decade and is well qualified to effectively communicate your organization’s financial condition and performance – we understand the importance of financial transparency. Our staff members are highly trained and have extensive knowledge and expertise in accounting procedures and processes for nonprofit organizations. Whether it’s preparing a month-end close, a budget-to-actual report, or creating best practices – we at ASNY understand the value of timely, accurate and reliable financial information.

Our Nonprofit Services
  • Bookkeeping, including weekly and month-end closing
  • General ledger setup and review
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Financial statement preparation
  • Business start-up consulting and management services
  • Preparation for financial audits, reviews and compilations
  • Internal-control and risk-management design and reviews
  • Accounts payable and accounts receivable
  • Payroll preparation
  • Bank reconciliations
  • Monthly closings, journal entries and general ledger postings
  • General ledger account reconciliation and analysis
  • Assistance in selecting and implementing accounting software
  • Implementing and documenting accounting department policies and procedures (fiscal policies and procedures manual)
  • Assistance with special projects
  • Preparation of consolidated fiscal reports
  • Cost report preparation
  • Federal form 990 tax return preparation
  • Guidance for governing board compliance with today’s “best practices”
